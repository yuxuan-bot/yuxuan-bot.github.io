👋🏻 About Me

I am currently a senior undergraduate student majoring in Software Engineering at the School of Software Engineering, Yunnan University, Kunming, China, under the supervision of Associate Professor Zhenli He. In September 2026, I will join the School of Software & Microelectronics at Peking University, Beijing, China, to pursue my graduate degree. My research interests mainly focus on AI Infra, Mobile Edge Computing, LLM Inference, and Reinforcement Learning, with the goal of designing efficient, scalable, and trustworthy intelligent systems.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I embrace a vibrant lifestyle and enjoy exploring the world. Here are some small but joyful hobbies I’ve picked up along the way:

  • ⛰️ Big fan of hiking and hitting the gym—keeps me sane and keeps my endurance up.
  • ⛷️ Learning to ski (total beginner here!), mostly trying not to fall while figuring out how to carve.
  • 🦐 I’ve low-key mastered the art of peeling shrimp perfectly intact. Super therapeutic —— I just love the process.
  • 📚 I'm an occasional reader—usually lose myself in a good detective or mystery novel.
  • 🍲 Total foodie. If there’s hotpot or BBQ involved, I’m there.
  • 💻 Love messing around with new tech and contributing to OSS projects whenever I can.
  • 🐾 Obsessed with cats and dogs, especially Ragdolls. They're just the best.

I am actively seeking collaborators of like-minded interests. If you are interested in my work, please feel free to contact me via email.
